\section{Appendix A: Supplementary Diagnostics Supporting the Core Claims}
\label{app:a}

This appendix includes only supplementary diagnostics that materially strengthen the interpretation of the core results reported in the main text. Its purpose is not to introduce new estimands, new policy regimes, or new empirical claims, but to reinforce the technical credibility of three central conclusions: censoring-aware temporal hazard reliability, mechanism-level interpretability of policy contrasts, and uncertainty-aware subgroup validation. By the end of this appendix, the reader should expect a compact set of supplementary figures that audit the assumptions and diagnostics underlying the main claims without expanding the substantive scope of the paper.

The appendix follows the same evidence contract used in the main text. Each retained figure must either validate a technical assumption required for interpretation, explain a central magnitude pattern, or audit the uncertainty of a reported structural contrast. Under that rule, Appendix~A retains four diagnostic blocks and excludes peripheral visuals that do not materially change the argument.

\subsection{Selection Rule for Supplementary Figures}

A figure is included in Appendix~A only if it satisfies at least one of the following conditions:
\begin{itemize}
\item it validates a technical assumption required to interpret the main RQ1--RQ3 findings;
\item it explains a key magnitude pattern reported in the main text;
\item it audits uncertainty for a reported structural contrast.
\end{itemize}

This rule defines the appendix boundary. The role of the appendix is therefore supportive rather than exploratory: it extends auditability, but it does not alter the interpretive contract of the paper.

\subsection{A.5 Appendix Boundary}

Appendix~A is intentionally constrained to diagnostics that materially support interpretation of the main claims. It does not introduce new estimands, new policy regimes, or new causal claims. Its function is to improve traceability, uncertainty auditing, and technical transparency for results already reported in the main text.

Accordingly, all conclusions remain within the same contract used throughout the paper: the reported quantities are \textit{model-implied simulated regime contrasts} under explicit protocol rules, and the appendix serves only to strengthen the evidentiary basis for reading those contrasts.
